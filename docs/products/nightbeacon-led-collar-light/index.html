Product FAQ
Can my dog swim with it?

No — IPX5 handles rain and splashes, but it should not be submerged.

How do I know when it’s charged?

The indicator glows red while charging and turns green when full (about 1 hour).

Does the light bother the dog?

It points outward, away from the eyes. Most dogs ignore it after the first minute.

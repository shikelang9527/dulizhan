Product FAQ
Will the adhesive damage my wall?

The included strips are the removable type. For textured walls we recommend the screw mount instead.

What height should I install it?

About the height of your cat’s cheek when standing — typically 20–25 cm from the floor.

My cat ignores it. Any tips?

Rub a little of your cat’s own scent onto it (a quick wipe with a cloth your cat sleeps on usually works).

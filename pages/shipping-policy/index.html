Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Processing time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 1-3 business days (Mon–Fri, excluding US public holidays). During promotions this may extend by 1–2 days — we'll note it at checkout when that happens.

Transit time

Standard tracked shipping takes an estimated 7-15 business days from dispatch. This is an estimate based on our carriers' typical performance, not a guarantee. Carrier delays, customs checks and weather can extend it.

Shipping rates

  • Orders over $35.00: FREE standard shipping
  • Orders under $35.00: flat $4.95

Destinations

We currently ship to: United States. We do not ship to PO boxes for some oversized items (noted on the product page), APO/FPO addresses, or US territories at this time.

Tracking

You'll receive a tracking number by email when your order ships. Tracking typically begins updating 2–5 days after dispatch as the parcel enters the carrier network. If your tracking hasn't moved for 7+ days, contact us and we'll open a carrier investigation.

Delays

If we cannot dispatch within the stated processing time, or transit meaningfully exceeds the estimate, we will notify you proactively and offer the choice to keep waiting or cancel undelivered items for a full refund, consistent with the FTC Mail Order Rule.

Duties & import fees

US orders under $800 de minimis typically incur no import charges; any duties or taxes, if assessed, are the buyer’s responsibility.

Lost or stuck parcels

If a parcel is confirmed lost by the carrier, or shows no movement for 20+ days, we reship free of charge or issue a full refund — your choice. Contact www.shikelang@gmail.com with your order number.

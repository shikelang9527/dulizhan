Product FAQ
Which size for my pet?

Measure your pet curled up and add ~15 cm. The M (60 cm) fits most cats and dogs up to 10 kg.

Does it flatten over time?

The PP-cotton fill can compress with heavy daily use. Fluff it after washing to restore loft.

Is the fur shed-proof?

A small amount of loose faux fur on first use is normal and stops after the first wash.

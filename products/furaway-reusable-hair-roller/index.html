Product FAQ
Does it work on clothes?

It is designed for larger fabric surfaces. For clothing, a small lint brush is more practical.

Does it pick up short fine fur?

Yes — short back-and-forth strokes work best for fine embedded fur.

Can I wash the roller?

Wipe the head with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Do not submerge in water.
